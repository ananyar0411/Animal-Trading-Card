Dog

A Dog can smell your feelings such as fear or grief 100,000 times better than humans.

  • Lifespan: 10-13 Years
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Needs: Food and Love
  • Nature: Friendly yet protective

A dog is a domestic animal and a popular companion or pets among humans. Dogs are mammals. Dog and man have been companions for almost 40,000 years and is a tamed animal. Dogs are said to be very loyal to their owners and make sure that they are safe at all times.
